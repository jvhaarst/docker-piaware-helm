PiAware has been deployed!

To verify the deployment, run:

  kubectl get deployment -n {{ include "piaware.namespace" . }} {{ include "piaware.fullname" . }}

To check the pods:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ include "piaware.namespace" . }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "piaware.name" . }}

To view logs:

  kubectl logs -n {{ include "piaware.namespace" . }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "piaware.name" . }}

To access the web interface:

  kubectl port-forward -n {{ include "piaware.namespace" . }} svc/{{ include "piaware.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.webPort }}

Then open http://localhost:8080 in your browser.

IMPORTANT: Make sure your Kubernetes nodes with RTL-SDR hardware are labeled:

  kubectl label node <node-name> rtlsdr=true
